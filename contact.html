Community

You can get in touch with us through Github to report a bug or submit a patch.

To discuss with other CoreObject developers, join the Étoilé mailing lists or IRC channel.

IRC

Meet CoreObject developers by visiting the Etoile channel on EFNet.

Mailing Lists

If you are interested in CoreObject, want to ask questions or contribute, please join our two main Étoilé mailing lists:

  • etoile-discuss – CoreObject users and developers
  • etoile-dev – CoreObject team and contributors

Team

CoreObject is developed and supported by a two-man team under the Étoilé project umbrella.

Eric Wasylishen

Eric is an Objective-C developer.

Quentin Mathé

Quentin is an Objective-C developer.

We would like to thank all the people who participated over the years, especially Yen-Ju Chen and David Chisnall who wrote early CoreObject prototypes, and Christopher Armstrong who more recently contributed code and ideas that helped to shape the current CoreObject version. We also would like to mention Nicolas Roard, Jesse Ross, Banlu Kemiyatorn and other people involved in Étoilé, who proposed early ideas, and took a role in CoreObject's birth in some way.
